Cocktails Finder is an application that allows users to easily search for a variety of cocktails. The app provides a compilation of popular and classic cocktail recipes.

The main feature of Cocktails Finder is advanced cocktail search. You can enter the name of a specific cocktail, the app presents the results, each cocktail is described along with a list of ingredients and instructions.
